Privacy Policy

Relaxing Rosie is a calm, simple website. You can browse without creating an account, and we do not sell your personal information.

This page explains what data may be collected when you visit, why it is collected, and what choices you have.

Information we collect

We do not ask you to provide personal information to view this site.

Like most websites, limited information may be collected automatically through standard web technologies and third-party services. This may include:

  • Device and browser information (for example, browser type or screen size)
  • Pages you visit and how long you stay
  • General location information (approximate, not a street address)
  • Referring pages (for example, if you came from YouTube or search)

Cookies and similar technologies

Cookies are small files that help websites function properly, understand traffic, and (if enabled) show ads.

Relaxing Rosie may use cookies and similar technologies provided by third parties, including Google, to display advertising and measure performance.

Google AdSense and advertising

If advertising is enabled on this site, Google and other third-party vendors may use cookies to serve ads based on your visits to this site and other websites.

You can learn more about how Google uses data and how to manage ad personalization by visiting Google’s advertising and cookie information .

How information may be used

Information collected through the site may be used to:

  • Keep the site running smoothly and securely
  • Understand what content is helpful (so we can make more of it)
  • Measure performance of pages and links
  • Support site maintenance through ads (if enabled)

Third-party links

This site may link to third-party platforms like YouTube or GitHub. Those platforms have their own privacy policies, and their practices are not controlled by this website.

Your choices

You may be able to control cookies through your browser settings. You can also use tools provided by Google to manage ad personalization if ads are enabled.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out through the contact page: Contact.

Updates

This policy may be updated occasionally. The latest version will always be posted on this page.
