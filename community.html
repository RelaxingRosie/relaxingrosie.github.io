Community

This is a shared space for people who love cats, care deeply about their homes, and value calm over noise. You don’t have to be loud, fast, or polished to belong here.

Relaxing Rosie began as a way to slow down and observe what actually works in real, multi-cat homes. This community exists for the same reason, to share thoughtfully, learn gently, and support one another without pressure.

Community discussions

Conversations live in our community discussions, hosted on GitHub. You’re welcome to ask questions, share observations, or simply read along.

Go to community discussions

What this space is for

  • Observations about multi-cat households and shared environments
  • Enrichment ideas that prioritize safety, choice, and calm
  • Questions you’re carrying or topics you want explored further
  • Quiet encouragement for people doing their best

A few gentle guidelines

  • Be kind and assume good intent
  • Share from your own experience rather than giving instructions or diagnoses
  • If someone shares something tender, respond with care, or simply listen
  • Disagreement is okay, cruelty or shaming is not

Where to begin

If you’d like an easy first step, consider introducing yourself in the discussions. You can share how many cats you live with, what brought you here, or what you’re currently learning.

You’re also welcome to read quietly for a while. Being here counts.
